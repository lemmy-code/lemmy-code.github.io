Privacy Policy for Merge Mob: Collect & Breed

Last updated: July 3, 2026

Introduction

Mizly (“we”, “our”, or “us”) operates the Merge Mob: Collect & Breed mobile application (the “Game”). This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected and how it is used when you play our Game.

Information We Collect

Advertising Data

Our Game displays advertisements through third-party ad networks. These networks may collect and use certain data to serve ads (including personalized ads where you have consented), including:

  • Advertising ID (Google Advertising ID)
  • Device information (model, operating system version)
  • IP address
  • General location (country/region level)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

Ad Networks Used

Our Game serves ads through Unity LevelPlay mediation, which includes the following networks:

  • ironSource / Unity LevelPlay — Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob — Privacy Policy
  • Unity Ads — Privacy Policy

Analytics & Diagnostics

We use Google Firebase to understand how the Game is used and to keep it stable:

  • Firebase Analytics — anonymous, aggregated usage and gameplay events (e.g. sessions, level progress, purchases) to improve the Game.
  • Firebase Crashlytics — crash and stability diagnostics.
  • Firebase Remote Config — to configure game features remotely.

This data is processed by Google. See Firebase’s Privacy and Security information.

Push Notifications

If you allow notifications, we use Firebase Cloud Messaging to send reminders (for example, when an egg is ready to hatch or a daily reward is available). To deliver these, your device’s notification token is stored on our backend service (hosted by Supabase). You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal information such as your name, email, or phone number.
  • We do not require account creation or login.
  • We do not track your precise location.
  • We do not sell your personal data.

In-App Purchases

Our Game offers in-app purchases processed through Google Play. Payment information is handled entirely by Google and is not accessible to us. See Google’s Privacy Policy for details.

Consent (European Economic Area, United Kingdom & Switzerland)

If you are located in the EEA, the UK, or Switzerland, we present a consent message before serving personalized ads, in accordance with Google’s EU User Consent Policy and applicable data-protection law (GDPR). You may grant or decline consent, and you can change your choice at any time via the privacy options in the Game. Where consent is declined, only non-personalized ads are shown.

Data Storage

Your game progress and settings are stored locally on your device. Analytics, diagnostics, and your notification token are processed by the third-party services described above; we do not otherwise store your personal data on our own servers.

Children’s Privacy

Our Game is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can remove it.

Your Rights

  • Reset your Advertising ID through your device settings.
  • Opt out of personalized ads through your device settings (Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization), or by declining consent where the consent message is shown.
  • Disable notifications in your device settings.
  • Delete local game data by uninstalling the Game.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above.
